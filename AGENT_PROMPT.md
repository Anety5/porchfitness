## üìã The 14 Porch Fitness Exercises

### SEATED EXERCISES (1-11)

#### 1. Seated Piriformis Stretch (Modified)
- Cross one ankle over opposite knee (figure 4 position)
- Gently press down on raised knee
- Sit tall, keeping back straight
- Hold 20-30 seconds each side
- **Benefits:** Hip flexibility, sciatica relief, lower back tension

#### 2. Seated Hamstring Stretch
- Sit on edge of chair, extend one leg straight
- Keep other foot flat on ground
- Reach toward extended toe, keep back straight
- Hold 20-30 seconds each side
- **Benefits:** Leg flexibility, posture

#### 3. Sit to Stands
- Sit upright, feet flat on ground
- Stand up without using hands (if safe)
- Slowly lower back down with control
- Repeat 10-15 times
- **Benefits:** Leg strength, balance, functional fitness

#### 4. Seated Cat-Cow Stretch
- Sit tall, hands on knees
- Inhale: Arch back, look up (Cow)
- Exhale: Round spine, tuck chin (Cat)
- Flow between poses 8-10 times
- **Benefits:** Back mobility, posture, breathing

#### 5. Seated Arm Raises
- Sit upright with good posture
- Raise both arms overhead slowly
- Lower with control
- Repeat 10-15 times
- Can hold light weights (water bottles)
- **Benefits:** Shoulder mobility, upper body strength

#### 6. Seated Ankle Circles
- Sit upright, engage your core
- Lift one foot off ground
- Rotate ankle slowly in circles
- Do 10 circles clockwise, then 10 counter-clockwise
- Switch feet and repeat
- **Benefits:** Ankle mobility, circulation, posture awareness

#### 7. Seated Side Bends
- Sit tall, raise one arm overhead
- Bend to opposite side, feel stretch along ribs
- Hold 15-20 seconds each side
- Keep hips on chair
- **Benefits:** Core flexibility, breathing capacity

#### 8. Seated Spinal Twist
- Sit tall, feet flat on ground
- Twist torso to one side
- Place hand on opposite knee or chair back
- Look over shoulder
- Hold 15-20 seconds each side
- **Benefits:** Spine mobility, digestion, posture

#### 9. Figure 4 Stretch (Deep Hip Opener)
- Cross ankle over opposite thigh (figure 4)
- Flex raised foot
- Gently lean forward from hips
- Hold 20-30 seconds each side
- **Benefits:** Deep hip stretch, glute flexibility

#### 10. Seated Shoulder Rolls
- Sit tall, arms relaxed at sides
- Slowly roll shoulders up, back, and down
- Make circles in both directions
- Repeat 8-10 times each direction
- **Benefits:** Shoulder tension relief, upper back mobility

#### 11. Seated Neck Stretches
- Sit tall, shoulders relaxed
- Gently tilt head to one side (ear toward shoulder)
- Hold 15-20 seconds each side
- Look down diagonally for deeper stretch
- **Benefits:** Neck tension relief, mobility

### STANDING EXERCISES (12-14)

#### 12. Standing Tree Pose
- Stand beside chair, hold for balance
- Place one foot on opposite ankle or calf (not knee!)
- Hold 20-30 seconds each side
- Focus on breathing
- **Benefits:** Balance, focus, leg strength

#### 13. Standing Calf Raises
- Stand behind chair, hold for balance
- Rise up on toes slowly
- Hold at the top for 2 seconds
- Lower with control
- Repeat 10-15 times
- **Benefits:** Ankle strength, balance, circulation, fall prevention

#### 14. Warrior I / Hip Flexor Stretch
- Stand behind chair for support
- Step one foot back into lunge position
- Keep back heel down or lifted
- Sink hips forward gently
- Hold 20-30 seconds each side
- **Benefits:** Hip flexor stretch, leg strength, balance
