Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

What We Collect

PorchFitness collects minimal data to provide you with personalized fitness coaching:

  • Exercise logs: Exercise names, pain levels, notes, reps, and duration
  • Account info: If you sign in with Google, we store your name and email
  • Voice interactions: Conversations with Samantha (ElevenLabs AI) are processed securely

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Track your workout progress over time
  • Generate personalized AI summaries and insights
  • Provide voice coaching tailored to your history
  • Send weekly progress emails (if you request them)

We never sell or share your data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

PorchFitness uses the following trusted services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and database storage
  • ElevenLabs: AI voice coaching (Samantha)
  • Google Gemini AI: Generating workout summaries

Each service has its own privacy policy. We recommend reviewing them:

  • Firebase Privacy
  • ElevenLabs Privacy
  • Google Privacy

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View all your workout logs in the Progress dashboard
  • Delete: Sign out and your anonymous data is isolated to your device
  • Export: Contact us to request a copy of your data

Data Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase's industry-standard encryption. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS. We follow best practices to protect your information.

Children's Privacy

PorchFitness is designed for adults and seniors. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
