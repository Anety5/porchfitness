# PorchFitness - ElevenLabs Accelerate Challenge

**Voice-Powered Chair Exercises for Seniors**

---

## Project Overview

PorchFitness is a voice-first web application that guides older adults through gentle chair exercises using ElevenLabs Conversational AI. No app downloads, no complicated interfacesâ€”just a warm, encouraging voice coach accessible from any device.

---

## Key Features

- **Voice-First Design** - Natural conversation with AI coach
- **14 Gentle Exercises** - 11 seated, 3 standing (with support)
- **Safety-First** - Built-in medical guardrails and reminders
- **Accessible Anywhere** - No app download required
- **Free to Use** - No subscription barriers

---

## Quick Start

1. Visit the website
2. Click any exercise card
3. Follow voice guidance from the AI coach
4. Move safely at your own pace

---

## Exercise List

### Seated (1-11)
1. Seated Piriformis Stretch (Modified)
2. Seated Hamstring Stretch
3. Sit to Stands
4. Seated Cat-Cow Stretch
5. Seated Arm Raises
6. Seated Ankle Circles
7. Seated Side Bends
8. Seated Spinal Twist
9. Figure 4 Stretch
10. Seated Shoulder Rolls
11. Seated Neck Stretches

### Standing (12-14)
12. Standing Tree Pose
13. Standing Calf Raises
14. Warrior I / Hip Flexor Stretch
